Privacy Policy (English)

Somali Market respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we store it, and your choices.

1. Who we are

Somali Market is a marketplace mobile application for buying and selling in Somalia. It is operated by Zakariya Adan Team.

Contact: somalimarket.help@gmail.com

Package name: com.somalimarket.app

2. Data we collect

  • Account: email address, display name, authentication provider (Google or email/password)
  • Seller profile: Somali phone number, hashed 4-digit PIN (never stored in plain text), verification status
  • Listings: titles, descriptions, photos, price, location, category, and related metadata
  • Messages: chat content between buyers and sellers about listings
  • Device & app settings: language preference (Somali/English), theme, and basic technical data needed for security and notifications
  • Payment-related data (when enabled): mobile money phone number used for in-app payments — processed through our secure server, not stored in the app binary

3. How we use data

  • To operate the marketplace: show listings, enable chat, calls, and saved ads
  • To verify sellers (phone + PIN) and enforce posting rules
  • To send one-time passwords (OTP) to your email for PIN or password reset via our Firebase Cloud Function and SMTP
  • To send push notifications for new messages when you allow notifications
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and fraud
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

4. Storage and security

  • Data is stored on Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage for images, Cloud Functions)
  • PINs are stored using a one-way hash with salt — we cannot read your PIN
  • Merchant payment API keys are stored server-side only; they are not embedded in the app
  • No system is 100% secure. Use a strong password and protect your device

5. Sharing

  • Other users see your public listing information and messages you send them in chat
  • Google Sign-In is subject to Google's Privacy Policy when you use that option
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to prevent fraud, harm, or abuse

6. Permissions

The app may request:

  • Camera & photos — to take or upload listing images
  • Microphone — optional voice messages in chat
  • Phone — to call a seller from a listing
  • Notifications — to alert you about new chat messages
  • Internet — required for all app features

7. Your rights

  • Request account help or data correction by emailing us
  • Delete your listings and sign out at any time
  • Change language and theme in Settings
  • Deny optional permissions (some features may not work)

8. Children

The app is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes may be communicated in the app or by email where appropriate.

Siyaasadda Sirta (Af-Soomaali)

Somali Market waxay ixtiraamtaa sirtaada. Siyaasadani waxay sharaxaysaa xogta aan ururinno, sababta, iyo xuquuqdaada.

1. Yaa annagu nahay

Somali Market waa app suuq online ah oo loogu talagalay iibinta iyo iibsiga Soomaaliya. Waxaa maamula Zakariya Adan Team.

Email: somalimarket.help@gmail.com

2. Xogta aan ururinno

  • Akoon: email, magac, Google ama email + password
  • Iibiyaha: telefoon Somali, PIN 4-digit (hash — lama kaydiyo qoraal cad)
  • Xayeysiis: cinwaan, sharaxaad, sawirro, qiime, goob, qaybta
  • Fariimo: sheekooyinka buyer iyo seller
  • Dejinta: luqad (Soomaali/Ingiriis), theme, ogeysiisyada

3. Sida loo isticmaalo

  • In app-ku shaqeeyo: muuji xayeysiis, sheeko, wicitaan
  • In la xaqiijiyo iibiyaha (telefoon + PIN)
  • In OTP loo diro email-kaaga (Forgot PIN / password) iyada oo loo marayo Firebase server
  • In laga hortago spam iyo khiyaano
  • Kama iibinno xogtaada shirkado kale

4. Kaydin iyo ammaan

  • Xogta waxaa lagu kaydiyaa Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Functions)
  • PIN-ka waa hash — ma akhrin karno PIN-kaaga
  • Furayaasha lacag bixinta waa server kaliya — ma jiraan app-ka gudihiisa

5. Ogolaanshaha app-ka

  • Kaamirada & sawirro — sawirrada xayeysiiska
  • Makarafoon — fariimaha codka (ikhtiyaari)
  • Telefoon — wicitaan iibiyaha
  • Ogeysiisyada — fariimaha cusub

6. Xuquuqdaada

  • Nala soo xiriir email haddii aad caawimaad u baahato
  • Tirtir xayeysiisyadaada oo ka bax app-ka markaad rabto
  • Beddel luqad iyo theme Settings-ka

7. Carruurta

App-ku looma talagalin dadka ka yar 13 sano.

8. Isbeddel

Waxaan cusboonaysiin karnaa siyaasaddan. Taariikhda "Last updated" ayaa isbeddeli doonta.
